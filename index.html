Improving the performance of high-tech hardware startups

Tec-Start is a consulting practice primarily targeted at improving the overall execution performance of high-tech hardware startups that require close coordination of multi-disciplinary teams.

This includes everything from critiquing the development plan and assessing the engineering team, to acting as temporary CTO or CEO.

Dan Meisburger, Ph.D. Principle

Consultant, entrepreneur, inventor and corporate executive with a proven track record of developing advanced lithographic and inspection equipment for the semiconductor and PCB industries and bringing them successfully to market.

25 patents awarded worldwide and 21 papers published in the fields of optical lithography, mask and wafer inspection, and electron beam systems.
